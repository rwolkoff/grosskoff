Ceremony

Maltz Performing Arts Center at Temple Tifereth Israel
1855 Ansel Road
Cleveland, OH 44106

The ceremony will be held at the Maltz Performing Arts Center at Temple Tifereth Israel. We recommend checking out their website, where you can learn more about the history of the building and take a virtual tour to see it before you come! For information about parking at the venue, click here.

Reception

St. Clair Ballroom
100 St. Clair NE
4th Floor, Key Center
Cleveland, OH 44114

Following the ceremony, the reception will held at the St. Clair Ballroom, attached to the Cleveland Marriott Downtown at Key Tower. To get to the ballroom, you may use the entrance located on St. Clair Ave. Valet parking provided at the St. Clair Ballroom entrance for non-hotel guests. If you are in the Marriott, go to the lobby and follow the signs for the St. Clair Ballroom.
