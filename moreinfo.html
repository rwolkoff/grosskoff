Transportation

Transportation Between Hotel, Ceremony, and Reception

Buses will be provided between the Cleveland Marriott Downtown at Key Tower (the hotel / reception) and the Maltz Performing Arts Center at the Temple-Tifereth Israel (the ceremony) for those who need/are interested on November 23rd. A schedule of bus times will be posted here closer to the wedding date, but expect buses to be leaving between 4:30 pm and 4:45 pm to arrive at the Maltz between 5:00 pm and 5:15 pm. Parking is also available at both venues.

Transportation Between Airport and Hotel

To get from the airport to the hotel, you can take a taxi, use ride-sharing apps Uber or Lyft, rent a car, or use Cleveland's public transportation - the RTA. It is about a 20 minute drive from the airport to the hotel or 40 minutes via the RTA (including walking time). If you happen to get in late at night Lyft may be slow. If you are renting a car, there will be a fee at the hotel for parking overnight.

To take the RTA, follow signs in the airport for the Red line, towards Stokes/Windermere. You'll get off at Tower City/Public Square station. It is a 10 minute walk from there to the hotel. The hotel and reception are right next to Public Square.
